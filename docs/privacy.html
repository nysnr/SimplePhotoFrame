Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 9, 2026

We value your privacy above all else. Our goal with PhotoFrame is to provide you with a beautiful way to enjoy your memories, with complete peace of mind.

Your Photos Stay With You

This application is designed to function entirely locally on your device. We access your photo library solely for the purpose of displaying your photos within the app's frames and slideshows.

We guarantee that your photos are never uploaded to our servers, nor are they shared with any third parties. Your memories remain private and secure on your own device.

Advertising and Data Usage

To maintain this application as a free service for everyone, we utilize Google AdMob to display advertisements.

Please note that AdMob may use anonymous identifiers and cookies to serve ads that are relevant to you. This process does not involve accessing your personal photos or identifiable personal information. By using this app, you kindly agree to this standard industry practice.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about your privacy, please do not hesitate to contact us. We are here to help.
